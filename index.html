Where I've Lived!

Bio

I'm from Savannah, Ga originally, but I've lived in North Carolina, Idaho, Montana, and I now currently reside in Madison, Wi. I've worked as a welder, warehouse manager, equipment operator, (most impressively "burrito roller") and now I work as a PML for Target. I received my BFA in Sound Design with a Minor in Music Production from the Savannah College of Art and Design in 2020. I took this path instead of moving to L.A. I'm hoping that this class adds to the pile of skills I've acquired over the last 11 years and maybe I'll be able to merge all of this knowledge into something cohesive. I recently got married in June of this year, and now my wife and I try to balance 3 fur babies and student loans!
